Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Long Home ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Long Home Confirmation Helper desktop application (the "Application").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

The Application is designed to assist with appointment confirmation calls. When you use the Application, you may enter the following types of information:

  • Appointment details (date, time, customer name, address, phone number)
  • Confirmation type (Roof or Bath)
  • Customer responses during the confirmation process

1.2 Local Storage

All information you enter into the Application is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of this information on our servers or third-party services.

2. How We Use Information

The Application uses the information you provide solely to:

  • Display script guidance during confirmation calls
  • Auto-fill appointment details in the confirmation script
  • Provide step-by-step guidance through the confirmation process

All processing occurs locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data entered into the Application is stored locally on your Windows device. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your local data, but you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The Application does not integrate with third-party services that collect or transmit your data. The Application operates entirely offline after the initial download.

5. Download and Installation

When you download the Application from our website, we may collect standard web analytics information such as:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Download timestamp
  • Browser type and version

This information is used solely for website analytics and is not linked to your use of the Application.

6. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • Delete the Application at any time, which will remove all locally stored data
  • Clear any locally stored data through your device's settings
  • Choose not to enter certain information into the Application

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support channels provided on our website.
