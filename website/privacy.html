Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your information

Last Updated: December 2024

Long Home ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Long Home Confirmation Helper desktop application (the "Application").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

The Application is designed to assist with appointment confirmation calls. When you use the Application, you may enter the following types of information:

  • Appointment details (date, time, customer name, address, phone number)
  • Confirmation type (Roof or Bath)
  • Customer responses during the confirmation process
  • Your name (stored locally for future use)

1.2 Local Storage

All information you enter into the Application is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of this information on our servers or third-party services. Your data stays on your device.

2. How We Use Information

The Application uses the information you provide solely to:

  • Display script guidance during confirmation calls
  • Auto-fill appointment details in the confirmation script
  • Provide step-by-step guidance through the confirmation process
  • Remember your preferences for future sessions

All processing occurs locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers or cloud services.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data entered into the Application is stored locally on your device using your operating system's local storage mechanisms. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your local data, but you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

The Application operates entirely offline after installation. No network connection is required for the Application to function, ensuring your data never leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The Application does not integrate with third-party services that collect or transmit your data. The Application operates entirely offline after the initial download.

We do not use analytics services, tracking cookies, or any other data collection mechanisms within the Application itself.

5. Download and Installation

When you download the Application from our website, we may collect standard web analytics information such as:

  • IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Download timestamp
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system type

This information is used solely for website analytics and improving our download service. It is not linked to your use of the Application and is not shared with third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • Delete the Application at any time, which will remove all locally stored data
  • Clear any locally stored data through your device's settings or by uninstalling the Application
  • Choose not to enter certain information into the Application
  • Access and review any data stored locally on your device

You have the right to request information about any data stored locally, though you can access this directly through your device's file system.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the support channels provided on our website.

We are committed to addressing any privacy concerns you may have and will respond to your inquiries in a timely manner.
